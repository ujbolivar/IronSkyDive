How do we structure our day?

1. Training

We teach all the necessary things to jump from the plane without any kind of problem.

2. Get ready

You are already prepared, you just need the suit and a parachute. All sizes available.

3. Fly

You are ready, an the plane is waiting for us in the hangar. Let's fly!

4. Jump!

You have done the most complex part. Just one step left...jump!
